
# Business Intelligence Requirements
The Business Intelligence module for the Microloan Approval and Risk Evaluation System focuses on delivering insights into borrower behavior, loan performance, and business health. BI will support decision-making through dashboards, KPIs, and analytical queries.

## BI Objectives
- Monitor loan approval trends.
- Evaluate risk distribution.
- Track repayment behavior.
- Detect potential defaults.
- Support strategic financial decisions.

## Data Sources
- Borrowers table
- Loans table
- Payments table
- Risk evaluations table
- Audit logs

## Analytical Focus
- Historical loan trends
- Risk level segmentation
- Borrower behavior patterns
- Payment compliance and delinquency rates
- Operational activity logs

## Output Formats
- Dashboards (Tableau/PowerBI/Looker)
- Aggregated reports
- Monthly summaries
- Automated KPI feeds
