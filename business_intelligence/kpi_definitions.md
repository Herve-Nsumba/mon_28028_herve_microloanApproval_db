
# KPI Definitions
Key Performance Indicators for the Microloan Approval & Risk Evaluation System.

## Loan KPIs
- **Loan Approval Rate:** (Approved loans / Total applications) * 100
- **Average Loan Size:** Mean loan amount approved
- **Loan Processing Time:** Average duration between application and approval

## Risk KPIs
- **Low/Medium/High Risk Distribution:** Percentage share of each risk level
- **Average Credit Score:** Mean credit score of evaluated loans
- **High-Risk Loan Ratio:** High-risk loans / Total loans

## Payment KPIs
- **Repayment Compliance Rate:** On-time payments / Total expected payments
- **Average Payment Amount:** Mean value per payment
- **Delinquency Rate:** Loans with late payments / Total active loans

## Operational KPIs
- **Audit Violations Count:** Blocked actions from triggers
- **User Activity Rate:** Number of allowed DML actions
